Gameplay

Gameplay of Fortnite

In each battle, the aim is to be the last one standing out of 100 players. As the game progresses,

the map that players battle on gets smaller and smaller. Players must collect weapons, build forts and shoot other players to defeat them

Fortnite also offers the opportunity to create custom maps and share them with others.
